About Dr. Richard Storm

Richard Storm is a patient-oriented and thorough ophthalmologist treating his patients in both Long Beach and Brooklyn, NY. Dr. Storm is certified by the American Board of Ophthalmology and the American Board of Pediatrics. Dr. Storm welcomes patients to visit his office to receive treatments that include eye muscle surgery, eyelid surgery, cataract surgery, lacrimal probing and irrigation for congenital tear duct blockage, and contact lens evaluation and fitting. He uses digital fundus photography to enhance the precision of his skill, and he also uses a phacoemulsification unit in his cataract surgeries. Dr. Storm attended the State University of New York's Downstate Medical Center to earn his medical degree. He prides himself on his highly personalized care and quality relationships with his patients. In his free time, Dr. Storm enjoys spending time with his wife as well as their children and grandchildren.
